Discover Our Mission
🏠

Orphanage Visits

The Launchpad Reach-Out Program runs regular orphanage visits that combine donation drives with mentorship, life-skills workshops, personal grooming sessions, and back-to-school support. We coordinate with care-home staff to deliver age-appropriate activities that promote dignity, confidence, and long-term development for children in residential care.

🍲

Street Outreach

Our mobile street outreach teams provide hot meals, hygiene kits, basic medical checks, and care packs to street children and people experiencing homelessness. Teams run health and hygiene awareness sessions, make referrals to local services and shelters, and scale up distributions during emergencies to meet urgent needs.

📦

Food Drives & Emergency Aid

We run regular food drives and seasonal giving projects (Christmas, Easter, winter care) alongside emergency response efforts to support families facing crisis. Our work combines immediate relief—delivering food, clothing, and essential supplies—with fundraising, community engagement, and partnerships to create sustainable, long-term impact.

🎓

Tutoring & Mentoring

Educational assistance is central to TLRP: peer-led tutoring, mentorship, life-skills development, and skills workshops. We inspire young volunteers to commit to service long-term while empowering children and youth with tools for education, personal development, and future opportunities.

🤝

How to Volunteer

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp.
  2. Attend a short orientation so we can match you to projects and explain safety guidelines.
  3. Join an event — bring any requested supplies and a positive attitude.

Safety: under-18s should be accompanied by a guardian. For activities with children we follow basic safeguarding and coordinate with local care providers.
